Our Story

Our founder Dr Leatile Dipheko, experienced firsthand the devastating consequences of an overstretched public healthcare system when he lost his father due to a shortage of doctors at a public hospital. His father could not receive help in time and that loss changed everything. Standing at that crossroads of grief and determination, Dr Dipheko made a vow that no family in Botswana should ever have to go through what his did. That vow became Dipheko Private Hospital.

What started as a promise grew into one of Botswana's most compassionate private healthcare facilities, located in the heart of Gaborone at Main Mall. From the very beginning, Dr Dipheko understood that quality healthcare should not be a privilege reserved for the wealthy. That is why Dipheko Private Hospital has a unique commitment patients from low income families are assessed and where possible, provided with care free of charge. We know where they come from because we come from there too.

In just five years, the hospital has grown to 58 wards, employed over 150 Batswana and serves hundreds of patients across four departments General Practice, Emergency, Cardiology and Maternity. Every member of our team shares the same fire that started this hospital a deep belief that every life matters and every patient deserves to be seen, heard and helped.

Our vision is simple but bold to save as many lives as possible and to become the best hospital in Botswana, while continuing to create meaningful employment for our people. We are not just building a hospital. We are building a legacy.
